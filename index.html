Cues can be any combination of physical, emotional, or mental stimuli that cause you to want to perform your habit routine. Common cues can be:

  • Time of day
  • Location
  • People
  • Emotional State
  • Sensation, Thought, Action

Routines are the actions or sequences of actions you perform automatically in response to your cue - as if your brain is on autopilot. This response is the habit you want to build!

Rewards represent the physical, mental or emotional satisfaction you receive after your routine has been completed. This is what motivates your brain to want to perform the routine again. Examples include:

  • Sense of accomplishment or progress
  • Feeling energized after eating healthy
  • Feeling rested after going to bed early
